1. Getting Started

Create your account and start using the platform

📝 Creating an Account

1
Visit the website and click Sign Up in the top navigation bar.
2
Enter your Full Name, Email Address, and a Password.
3
Re-enter your password in the Confirm Password field.
4
Click Create Account. You will be logged in automatically.

🔒 Signing In

1
Click Sign In in the navigation bar.
2
Enter your Email and Password.
3
Click Sign In. Based on your role, you'll be redirected to the appropriate dashboard.
✅ Role-Based Access
Customers go to the Home page · Vendors go to Vendor Dashboard · Admins go to Admin Dashboard

💻 Navigation Overview

Desktop

Use the top navigation bar. After login, click your name to see a dropdown menu with quick links to Orders, Addresses, Wallet, Support, and Profile.

Mobile

Use the bottom navigation bar for quick access to Home, Readymades, Cart, and Account. The hamburger menu provides additional options.

🛒

2. Customer Guide

Shop, order, and manage your account

🔍 Browsing Products

1
Click Readymades in the navigation bar to see all available products.
2
Use filters and search to find specific items by category, price, or name.
3
Click any product to view details, images, variants (size/color), and pricing.

🛒 Adding to Cart & Checkout

1
On the product page, select your size and color, then click Add to Cart.
2
Click the Cart icon in the navbar. Adjust quantities or remove items.
3
Click Proceed to Checkout. Select or add a delivery address.
4
Review your order summary and click Place Order.

💳 Payment Options

💳

Razorpay

Pay securely with UPI, Debit/Credit Cards, Net Banking, or Mobile Wallets. A payment window opens automatically.

💰

Wallet Balance

Use your wallet balance to pay fully or partially. Remaining amount can be paid via Razorpay.

📦 Tracking Your Orders

1
Click your name in the navbar, then select My Orders.
2
See all orders with their current status at a glance.
3
Click any order to view full details — items, payment info, shipping status, and timeline.

Order Status Guide

🚚 Shipping with Shiprocket

The platform integrates with Shiprocket for shipping. Follow these 3 steps in order:

① Create Shipment
→
② Assign AWB
→
③ Schedule Pickup
StepWhat Happens
Create ShipmentGenerates a shipment in Shiprocket with order and address details
Assign AWBGets a tracking number (Airway Bill) from the courier partner
Schedule PickupArranges for the courier to collect the package from your location
⚠️ Important
Each step must be completed in order. The next button becomes available only after the previous step is done.

💰 Commission Settings

Control how much commission the platform earns from each sale:

1
Go to Commissions in the sidebar.
2
Set a Global Default commission percentage (applies to all vendors).
3
Optionally set vendor-specific overrides for individual vendors.
4
Configure commission slabs for tiered rates based on order value.
🛈 How Commission Works
System checks: Vendor-specific rate → Global default → Fallback 5%. Commission is automatically deducted from each order.

5. FAQ & Policies

Common questions and platform policies

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Contact support through the Support section or the Contact page for password reset assistance.
Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Delivery timelines depend on your location and the vendor's shipping arrangement. Check the Shipping Policy for details.
Q: Can I cancel an order?
A: Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped. Go to My Orders, select the order, and request cancellation.
Q: How do refunds work?
A: Approved refunds are credited to your wallet balance, which can be used for future orders.
Q: How do I become a vendor?
A: Visit the Vendor Onboarding page, fill in your business details, upload required documents, and wait for admin approval.
Q: How is vendor commission calculated?
A: Commission is automatically deducted from each order. The admin sets rates (vendor-specific or global default, minimum 5%).
Q: How do vendor payouts work?
A: Vendors request payouts from their wallet. The admin reviews and approves the request. Approved amounts are transferred to the vendor's registered bank account.

Platform Policies

The following policies are available from the website footer:

  • Privacy Policy — How user data is collected, stored, and used
  • Terms and Conditions — Platform usage terms and agreements
  • Refund Policy — Refund eligibility criteria and process
  • Return Policy — Return eligibility criteria and process
  • Shipping Policy — Shipping timelines, charges, and delivery information
